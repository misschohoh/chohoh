ABOUT ME

I am currently living in Vancouver BC. I've also lived in many other cities along the west coast. The last place I lived was Los Angeles where I worked on plenty of amazing Entertainment and E-Commerce projects. I have a very diverse resume. I've worked on Small contracts for friends/Small businesses, Large contract jobs for one specific project, or working fulltime at agencies and Large companies.

I specialize in HTML5, CSS3, Jquery, JavaScript, Accessibility, Usability and Responsive Design. I've also assisted in the design, development and creation of web sites for small businesses, social networking platforms, client and server side businesses. I work hard to ensure timely and seamless integration of new developments from creation, design, development and documentation. My experience includes several different Content Management Systems (magento, documentum, drupal, docfactory, wordpress).

I have hands-on experience with HTML5, CSS3 JSON, HAML, SASS, PHP, XML, JavaScript, Photoshop, Illustrator, Git, SVN, LESS. My knowledge of object oriented programming and Jquery helps me to understand the big picture in the client/server-side relationship that Web 2.0 technologies cultivate. I am detail orientated, experienced in building out standards compliant, cross-browser compatible sites with a keen visual design style. She is up-to-date with current technologies and frameworks (grunt.js, node.js, backbone.js, Ember.js, Prototype, script.aculo.us, Mootools, Jquery).. so many other random tools I’ve picked up along the way.
